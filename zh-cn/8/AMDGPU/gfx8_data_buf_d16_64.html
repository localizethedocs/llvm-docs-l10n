vdata¶

16-bit data to store by a buffer instruction.

Size: depends on GFX8 GPU revision:

  • 2 dwords for GFX8.0. This H/W supports no packing.

  • 1 dword for GFX8.1+. This H/W supports data packing.

Operands: v
